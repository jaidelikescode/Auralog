Features

Everything you need to find the soundtrack of your life.

Auralog turns your moods, journal entries, and music taste into emotional insight, reflection, and playlists that actually feel personal.

🎧

AI Soundtrack Generator

Write something, say something, or do a quick check-in, and Auralog builds a playlist that matches the emotional vibe of that moment.

You can let yourself feel it out, or ask the app to counter your mood and lift your spirits instead.

😊

Mood Logging That Feels Real

Emotions are complicated, so the app doesn’t make you flatten them into one neat little box. You can log multiple moods at once, rate the intensity, and add notes if you want.

Keep it quick with tags or go deeper with a full entry.

🎵

Connect Your Music

Auralog connects to Spotify, Apple Music, or YouTube Music so it can get a real sense of what you actually like listening to.

It can pull from your own library, help you discover new artists, or do a mix of both.

📈

Emotional Insights Over Time

The app keeps track of your entries, listening habits, and emotional patterns so you can start noticing trends you might’ve missed otherwise.

Think moods by week, recurring songs, time-of-day energy shifts, and bigger-picture changes over time.

📝

Journal Your Way

You can write a full journal entry, record an audio journal, or use a quick check-in if you don’t have the energy to do a whole lot.

Need help getting started? Auralog can also give you prompts when your brain knows there’s a feeling there but doesn’t know how to word it yet.

🌐

Private, Public, or Somewhere In Between

Keep everything to yourself on a personal page, share playlists and posts with friends, collaborate, or make your entries public if that’s part of your vibe.

And at the end of the year, Auralog gives you a customized soundtrack built from your emotional journey across the calendar year.

How It Works

The process is simple. The emotional damage is optional.

1

Log How You Feel

Choose mood tags, write a journal entry, or record a voice note based on what kind of check-in you need that day.

2

Connect Your Music

Link your Spotify, Apple Music, or YouTube Music so Auralog understands your taste and what songs already matter to you.

3

Get a Soundtrack Back

The app turns your moment into a playlist, gives you reflection prompts if needed, and helps you notice patterns as your story unfolds.

Your life already has a soundtrack.

Auralog just helps you hear what it’s been trying to say.
